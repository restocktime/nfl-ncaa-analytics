ESPN Fantasy Football

Connect using league ID and cookies

How to get cookies (private leagues):
• Login to ESPN Fantasy
• Open browser Dev Tools (F12)
• Go to Application → Cookies → espn.com
• Copy espn_s2 and SWID values
